The Quilt

The morning sunlight fell through the tall stained-glass windows of the old church, scattering reds, blues, and golds across the wooden pews...

Daniel had grown up here. As a boy, he had sung in the choir...

That morning, the pastor’s voice echoed in the nearly empty sanctuary...

“Sometimes we make things not for the world to see, but for God to see.”

Daniel nodded, letting the words settle deep inside him...

When the quilt was finally finished, Daniel helped carry it...
