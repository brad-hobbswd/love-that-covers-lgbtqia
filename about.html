ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Bradley Hobbs is a writer, teacher, and advocate for healing, grace, and inclusion. With a heart for those who have felt unseen or unloved, he has dedicated his work to creating stories that inspire hope, restoration, and the understanding that God’s love reaches every heart.

Brad holds a degree in Information Technology and has over six years of experience in teaching and mentoring children and adults alike. Through personal experiences of faith, rejection, and healing, he has developed a deep understanding of the power of God’s love to restore and transform lives.

When not writing, Brad enjoys reading scripture, reflecting on life’s lessons, and encouraging others to embrace their identity and worth in God. His work, including Love That Covers, is dedicated to showing that love truly covers all and that everyone is chosen, seen, and deeply loved by God.
