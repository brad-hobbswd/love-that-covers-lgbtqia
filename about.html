About the Author

Bradley Hobbs is a writer, teacher, and advocate for healing, grace, and inclusion. With a heart for those who have felt unseen or unloved, his work speaks into places of silence and restores a sense of belonging through truth and compassion.

Brad holds a degree in Information Technology and brings over six years of experience teaching and mentoring both children and adults. His journey through faith, rejection, and healing has shaped a message centered on restoration and the unchanging love of Jesus.

Time spent in scripture, reflection, and encouragement continues to guide his work. Through Love That Covers, he invites others to embrace identity, dignity, and the assurance that they are fully seen and deeply loved.

You are seen. You are known. You are loved without condition.
